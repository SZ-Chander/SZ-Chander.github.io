## Who's me?
My name is CHANG Shunzhi. I was born in Canton, Guangdong, China and currently reside in Chiba Japan.  
I began my undergraduate studies in 2019 and graduated in 2023 with a Bachelor's degree. Later that same year, I moved to Japan to pursue further education. After completing nine months of Japanese language training — during which I advanced more quickly than expected — I began my Master’s program and obtained my Master of Engineering degree in 2026. And right now, I am currently working at a company in Japan.  
## Extracurricular Activities
During my undergraduate years, I founded a university debate team and trained more than ten members. Several of them went on to receive awards in university-level and regional debate competitions.  
I also participated in several entrepreneurship competitions and industry research projects. Although I did not ultimately launch a startup, these experiences allowed me to gain hands-on engineering-level development experience as well as practical experience collaborating with companies.  
## Interests
While my current professional role is no longer directly related to software development, I continue to treat development as a personal interest. I regularly build small projects for my own use, including lightweight AI applications for home use and practical tools such as personal finance trackers.  
Beyond software development, I have a strong interest in history and language learning. My historical interests focus particularly on European history from the Middle Ages to 1848, as well as the history of the Pacific War.
